Tic Tac Toe is a classic two-player game where players take turns marking spaces in a 3x3 grid with their respective symbols, typically X and O. The objective is to be the first player to align three of their symbols horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. The game begins with an empty grid, and players choose a space to mark during their turn. If a player successfully places three of their symbols in a row, they win the game. If all nine spaces are filled without any player achieving three in a row, the game ends in a draw. Tic Tac Toe is a game of strategy and foresight, where players must block their opponent's moves while creating opportunities to win.
